# Find a Launched and ready to go community created Commons solution that you can use today!

**Welcome to the Commons Resource Hub**

Your one-stop shop for volunteer-created and community-supported Salesforce apps, tools, and resources. All free and built by the community, for the community.

**:package: Apps & Packages**

**Declarative Lookup Rollup Summaries (DLRS)**

*Automate rollups across lookup relationships without code — trusted by 30,000+ orgs since 2013*

Keep Salesforce parent records up to date with totals and summaries. DLRS offers more flexibility than standard rollups, supports standard and custom lookup relationships, and helps surface key insights by keeping data automatically summarized.

* **Link:** [Install from AppExchange](https://appexchange.salesforce.com/appxListingDetail?listingId=a0N3000000B45gWEAR)
* **Audience:** All Salesforce customers and partners
* **Key Features:** Rollup across lookup relationships, declarative UI, filter criteria, real-time or scheduled modes
* **Resources:** [Documentation](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/DLRS-Documentation/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/SFDO-Community/declarative-lookup-rollup-summaries) | [Community Support](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/trailblazer-community/groups/0F9300000009O5pCAE?tab=discussion)
* **Note:** Volunteer-maintained • Used by 30,000+ customers • Active since 2013

**Summit Events App (SEA)**

*Flexible event management for registrations, payments, reporting, and more*

A sustainable open source events app serving a broad variety of needs for EDUs and nonprofits. Manage paid, free, group, or individual events through their full lifecycle: registration, receipting, confirmation, reporting, and web-facing exposure.

* **Link:** [Install via Salesforce](https://install.salesforce.org/products/SummitEventsApp/latest)
* **Audience:** All Salesforce customers and partners
* **Key Features:** Multiple event types, full event lifecycle management, compatible with Salesforce.org Data Architectures
* **Resources:** [Installation Guide](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/summit-events-app-documentation/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/SFDO-Community-Sprints/Summit-Events-App/wiki) | [Release Notes](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/summit-events-app-documentation/docs/release-notes/) | [Community Support](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/trailblazer-community/groups/0F94S000000kHi2?tab=discussion)
* **Note:** Volunteer-maintained • Active since 2018

**Salesforce Indicators**

*Simple, visual, and customizable data insights for your Salesforce records*

Visually highlight important data at a glance with this Custom Metadata-driven Lightning Web Component. Make key details pop with colorful, branded indicators on your Lightning pages — no code required.

* **Link:** [Install from AppExchange](https://appexchange.salesforce.com/appxListingDetail?listingId=192aeb3a-1476-4028-a25c-954d48560eba)
* **Audience:** All Salesforce customers and partners
* **Key Features:** Visual data display, custom colors and branding, no-code customization with Custom Metadata
* **Resources:** [Documentation](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/indicators-documentation/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/SFDO-Community/Salesforce-Indicators) | [Release Notes](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/indicators-documentation/docs/release-notes/) | [Community Support](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/trailblazer-community/groups/0F94S000000HEDASA4)

**Grassroots Mobile Survey (GRMS)**

*Offline-capable mobile surveys for field staff and volunteers*

Built for nonprofits with staff or volunteers in the field. Customizable, free, and open source. Download surveys to fill out offline, then sync when connectivity is restored — perfect for remote locations or disaster response.

* **Link:** [Install via Salesforce](https://install.salesforce.org/products/grms/latest)
* **Audience:** Nonprofit Salesforce customers
* **Key Features:** Offline and online capability, customizable surveys, works on iOS and Android
* **Resources:** [Install & Configuration](https://github.com/SFDO-Community/GrassrootsMobileSurveyApp/wiki/Salesforce-Initial-Setup) | [Learn More](https://github.com/SFDO-Community-Sprints/GrassrootsMobileSurveyApp/wiki) | [GitHub](https://github.com/SFDO-Community/GrassrootsMobileSurveyApp/wiki) | [Community Support](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/trailblazer-community/groups/0F94S000000kI60SAE?tab=members&sort=LAST_MODIFIED_DATE_DESC)
* **Note:** Volunteer-maintained • Free for nonprofits

**Sticky Selectron**

*Persistent multi-record selection in Screen Flows*

A Data Table Lightning Web Component that allows easy selection of multiple records in a screen flow, with selections persisting across flow screens. Perfect for complex admin workflows.

* **Link:** [Install via Salesforce](https://install.salesforce.org/products/sticky-selectron/latest)
* **Audience:** Nonprofit Salesforce customers
* **Key Features:** Load hundreds of records, sort and search, persistent selection across screens
* **Resources:** [Documentation](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/sticky-selectron-documentation/docs/configuration/) | [GitHub](https://github.com/SFDO-Community/sticky-selectron) | [Release Notes](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/sticky-selectron-documentation/docs/release-notes/) | [Community Support](https://trailhead.salesforce.com/trailblazer-community/groups/0F94S000000HDS7SAO)

**Charitable Receipting**

*A comprehensive guide to compliant tax receipting for US and Canadian nonprofits*

Tax receipting is a cornerstone of nonprofit financial management. This guide covers legal compliance, donor stewardship, acknowledgments vs. receipts, and key differences between CRA and IRS requirements.

* **Link:** [Read the Guide](https://sfdo-community-sprints.github.io/docs/Commons%20Solutions/launched/Charitable-Receipting/)
* **Audience:** Nonprofit organizations and Salesforce partners
* **Key Topics:** Legal compliance, audit preparation, donor stewardship, US vs. Canadian requirements, receipting solution comparison
* **Note:** Written at September 2025 Vancouver Community Sprint • 100% volunteer-maintained
